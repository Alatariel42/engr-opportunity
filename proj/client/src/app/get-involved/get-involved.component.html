Volunteer With Us!

Do you enjoy working with kids and teens? Do you care about STEM enrichment opportunities? Come join us!

As a volunteer, you'll have the opportunity to facilitate workshops at any of our partner organizations, gain a deeper understanding of engineering fundamentals, improve communication & teaching skills, and attend monthly volunteer outings & a bi-annual volunteer celebration.

All volunteers are required to attend a 90 minute training session before volunteering with us, and no technical experience is required! If you'd like more information about volunteering, please fill out our volunteer interest form and our Volunteer Coordinator will be in touch with you shortly.

Partner With Us!

We work with each partner organization to create a workshop structure and curriculum that works for them. All materials are supplied by us, and we can host workshops in any indoor space hosted by the partner organization.

If you're involved with a community organization or nonprofit that works with children between the ages of 8-18 that would be interested in working with us, please contact our Partner Coordinator, Sol Khaitas (skhaita@ncsu.edu).
